About LCU

LCU was created on December 27th, 2023. LCU started out with 2 people, JustPhantom50#7764 and legendlinko#5284 and has now grown to a team of five. LCU is a fully customizable bot for Emergency Response:Liberty County roleplay servers, we allow you to do things such as sessions, shutdowns, session votes and much much more, each embed is 100% customizable so you can set them up to your liking.
